About Mentor Mole

Mentor Mole is an educational search tool that is free for all to use. We aim to streamline the process of learning and self-teaching for all students.

  • Educational search engine that finds free courses, tutorials, and resources
  • Community of scholars and students who recommend and rate resources
  • All features are completely free thanks to community support

Experience simple, seamless studies with Mentor Mole!
