About Us

We are a group of computer science students at UTSA. Mentor Mole started out as a simple school project, but then grew to a full-fledged passion project.
Our goal is to simplify the process of learning for all students, and to help underserved communities who may not have easy access to educational resources/databases.
Reach out to us at contact@mentormole.com.
